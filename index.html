Neobrutalism

Thick borders, hard shadows, flat color fills, and deliberately raw typography. Loud and intentional.

Bento Grid

Modular card grids with variable sizing. Popularized by Apple — flexible, scannable, and marketing-friendly.
3D & Depth

Claymorphism

Soft, inflated 3D shapes with inner glow and pastel hues. Friendly, tactile, and surprisingly versatile.

Near-black surfaces, tight type scales, and one high-contrast accent. No clutter, maximum focus.

Typography

Type Pairings for 2026

Display + Body

Space Grotesk

Paired with Inter for body text. Technical, geometric, and confident. Common in SaaS and dev tools.

Space Grotesk (500–700) Inter (400–500)

Serif Revival

Editorial New

Serif display type is back in editorial and consumer apps. Contrasts beautifully with clean sans body text.

Editorial New (Italic) Inter (400)

Mono Futurism

JetBrains

Monospace display type signals precision, AI, and tech. Perfect for retro-futurist and terminal aesthetics.

JetBrains Mono (400–700) Inter (400)
